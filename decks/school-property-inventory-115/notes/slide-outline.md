# Slide Outline

## 1. Cover

- Title: `115年財產盤點準則`
- Subtitle: 校內財產盤點、檢查與後續處理作業說明
- Goal: establish this as a formal internal administrative deck

## 2. Basis And Purpose

- Why this work is being done
- Main legal and operational basis
- Main management goals

## 3. Scope And Property Types

- Which units are covered
- Four main property categories
- Clarify that this is school-wide and not limited to one office

## 4. Core Operating Principles

- At least one inventory and one inspection each year
- Must work from approved plans
- Must verify account, object, custodian, and location
- Must produce result reports and follow-up improvements

## 5. Responsibility Split

- General affairs office
- Offices and custodians
- Inspection group
- Academic affairs office

## 6. 115 Yearly Timeline

- March preparation
- April launch
- End of April printed lists distributed
- July 31 self-check deadline
- August 31 on-site inspection deadline
- September onward for corrections and follow-up

## 7. Inventory Procedure

- Approved plan
- Print lists and forms
- Unit-by-unit check
- Custodian sign-off
- Return results
- Consolidated report
- Follow-up tracking

## 8. Inspection Procedure

- Approved inspection plan
- On-site inspection
- Check labels, location, movement, use, maintenance, disposal, transfer
- Produce report and corrective actions

## 9. Exception Handling And Disposal

- Account mismatch
- Inventory gain / loss
- Idle asset
- Damaged or unusable asset
- Custodian or location mismatch
- Disposal is a separate process and cannot be replaced by inventory notes alone

## 10. Records And Closing Reminder

- Required retained records
- Implementation reminder
- Follow latest superior authority guidance if rules are updated
